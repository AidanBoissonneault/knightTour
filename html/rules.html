RULES

STANDARD MODE

A standard Knights Tour. Play as a chess knight and land on every square exactly once. A knight moves 2 squares in one direction and 1 squares in an adjacent direction from a knights edge.

INCREMENT

Follows the same play rules as STANDARD MODE Start with a 5x5 board. Each time the board is completed the game resets and a bigger board is made.

MULTIPLAYER

Two knights play on a shared knights tour. Both knights alternate moves, with white starting first. If a knight runs out of moves, or is captured by the opposing knight, they lose the match. In online multiplayer, the host is always white.
